About Andrea

The artist behind the canvas

[Artist Photo]

Based in Venice Beach, California, Andrea Estep is a contemporary artist whose work explores the intersection of color and emotion. With a background in both traditional painting and modern photography, she creates pieces that resonate with viewers on a deeply personal level.

Her paintings are characterized by bold brushwork and vibrant color palettes, while her photography captures moments of stillness and beauty in the everyday world.

Each piece is created with meticulous attention to detail and a passion for artistic expression.
